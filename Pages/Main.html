Welcome to:

O Chosen Undead, you have stumbled into the world of DARK SOULS.
Where you, oh chosen undead will experience a third person action RPG game.
This world is punishing and you will be challenged by the very molecules of this world.
Prepare to die and make mistakes, as you embark on a journey filled with peril, discovery, and triumph.
And finally relink the first flame or.. snuff it out...

Your appearance remains a mystery to be unfolded by you.
It is your decision to decide how you look and what kind of person you want to be in this wreteched world.

The tools at your disposal for creating your character are nigh endless.

So let your imagination run wild and create the character of your dreams.

With the classes you can choose from, you can be a knight, a sorcerer, a thief, or even a pyromancer.

Each class has its own unique abilities and playstyle, allowing you to tailor your experience to your liking.

Meet new allies.

Or meet new oppositions.

And lastly hone your skills.

Only the patient and the determined will prevail in this world.
You will die, and you will die a lot.
But do not despair, for each death is a lesson learned.
You will learn the patterns of your enemies, the layout of the world, and the secrets hidden within.
With each death, you will grow stronger and more skilled.
You will learn to adapt and overcome the challenges that lie ahead.
Now chosen undead, it is time to embark on your journey.
The world of DARK SOULS awaits you, filled with danger and wonder.
